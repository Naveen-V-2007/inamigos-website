Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation is a Central Government licensed non-profit organization, founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. The name "InAmigos" means "In Friends" — built on the belief that friendship, compassion, and unity can transform society.

From feeding the hungry to educating children, empowering women to protecting animals, and conserving nature to building careers — our six flagship projects are creating real, lasting change across India.

Backed by 80G, 12A, NITI Aayog, CSR-1 and ISO 9001:2015 certifications, we operate with full transparency and accountability. Every contribution goes directly towards building a more inclusive, compassionate and empowered society.

Our Projects

Food & Clothing

Project Seva

Providing food and clothing to underprivileged communities across India. Over 50,000+ meals distributed to those in need.

Education

Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children in rural areas through digital literacy and life skills.

Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence. 900+ women trained across 3 states.

Environment

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation through tree-planting drives, clean-up campaigns and sustainability workshops.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Rescue, protection and feeding of stray and injured animals across communities all over India.

Skill Development

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through internships and skill programs. 30,000+ interns trained across India.
